Lisa Wallace Deen is a life-long artist. She creates paintings, drawings, and mixed media work using a variety of styles and techniques. She finds inspiration in the sensory experience of earth, the patterns of plants, the effects of wind and water, the signs of decay and renewal. She expresses these interactions with the natural world through a unique combination of realism and abstraction.
